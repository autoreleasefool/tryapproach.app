Privacy Policy

Approach takes your privacy seriously. We do not collect any personally identifiable information about any of our users.

The app uses TelemetryDeck to collect anonymized analytics on the usage of the app for the sole purpose of improving the product. Their privacy policy can be found here. You can opt-out of analytics at any time through the app's settings.

The app also uses RevenueCat, which captures some analytics for in-app purchases and subscriptions. If you make an in-app purchase, RevenueCat receives some information to validate the purchase, but none of your payment information. They do not receive your credit card details. You can find RevenueCat's privacy policy here.

Approach does not sell or rent any of your data, and any anonymous information collected is used only to improve the app.
